# Interview II - Questions

## Introduction

First, introduce the app and let the user play with the MVP.

**Example introduction:**

**Mission of the app:** Fashion brands now produce nearly twice as much clothing as they did before 2000. The industry generates over 92 million tonnes of waste annually. However, studies reveal that nearly 25% of clothing in closets is never used. Environmental harm and financial waste result from underutilization. Rewear helps users make the most of their current wardrobes by turning wardrobe tracking into a quick daily routine that shows wear patterns and recommends items they may have forgotten.

---

## Questions

1. After seeing the app, which features feel most useful to you?
2. How intuitive was navigating through the app on first impression?
3. Would you actually log what you wear each day, and how often do you think you'd realistically use it?
4. What would make logging your outfits easier or more appealing?
5. Does the visual representation of your wardrobe help you see what you own?
6. Would you want recommendations on what to wear based on your logging data?
7. What information about your clothing habits would be most valuable to see?
8. Are there any features you expected to see that are missing?
9. Would you share your sustainability impact or clothing data with friends?
10. What would prevent you from using this app regularly?
11. Any last feedback or questions?
